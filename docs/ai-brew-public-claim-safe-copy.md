# AI Brew Public Claim Safe Copy

Use this language in product, release notes, and store copy.

## Approved Copy

AI Brew membuat resep awal yang kuat berdasarkan alat seduh, grinder, air, roast, proses, varietas, dan target rasa. Gunakan feedback rasa untuk koreksi satu variabel dulu.

AI Brew creates a strong starting recipe and dial-in guide based on method, grinder, water, roast, process, variety, and taste target. Refine it from real taste feedback.

AI Brew memisahkan air panas dan es untuk seduh dingin, menjaga batas aman Hario Switch, dan memberi peringatan ketika data grinder, air, atau bean belum cukup kuat.

## Avoid

- Claims that every bean will taste ideal.
- Claims that the system physically brewed every generated recipe.
- Claims that espresso is guaranteed without real dial-in.
- Claims that water labels are laboratory-verified unless the source provides that evidence.
- Claims that AI Chat may override deterministic recipe guardrails.

## Required Disclaimer

Prediksi rasa adalah estimasi barista, bukan jaminan. Seduhan pertama dipakai untuk membaca grind, suhu, air, teknik, dan koreksi berikutnya.
