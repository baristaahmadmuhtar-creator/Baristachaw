# AI Brew Production Claims

AI Brew is production-ready as a smart, guardrailed starting-point and dial-in assistant across many coffee beans and brewing methods. It does not guarantee perfect results for every bean; confidence labels, warnings, and taste-feedback corrections are provided to keep recommendations honest and safe.

## Bahasa Indonesia

AI Brew siap produksi sebagai titik awal cerdas dengan guardrail dan sebagai asisten dial-in untuk banyak beans dan metode seduh. AI Brew bukan jaminan hasil sempurna untuk setiap bean; label keyakinan, peringatan, dan feedback rasa disediakan agar rekomendasi tetap jujur dan aman.

## What AI Brew Can Claim

- Recipe math is deterministic and guarded by method constraints.
- Hario Switch plans validate closed-chamber load for Switch 02, Switch 03, and MUGEN x SWITCH.
- V60 hot and iced calibration is protected by regression tests.
- Unknown or partial bean data uses a conservative baseline and visible confidence.
- Risky beans, water, grinder, or method combinations show warnings instead of overclaiming accuracy.
- Taste feedback is the recommended correction loop after brewing.

## What AI Brew Must Not Claim

- It is not a perfect guarantee for every bean, grinder, water source, roast date, or pouring technique.
- Curated recipes are not official recipes unless a source explicitly says so.
- Water and grinder recommendations are not laboratory verification unless marked as measured or official.
- AI Coach does not replace deterministic recipe guardrails and must not silently change protected recipe numbers.

## Required Public Language

Use this wording when source and public production checks pass but universal accuracy cannot be guaranteed:
