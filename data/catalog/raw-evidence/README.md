# Raw Evidence

Phase-1 canonical catalog is bootstrapped from the existing AI Brew source-backed assets, then promoted into `data/catalog/normalized/phase1/`.

Rules for this folder:

- Store source captures, product notes, or regulator extracts before they become canonical rows.
- Do not publish an item from raw evidence directly.
- Move only verified rows into `normalized/phase1/`.
- Keep unofficial or partial findings in the review queue until they have a defensible source trail.
