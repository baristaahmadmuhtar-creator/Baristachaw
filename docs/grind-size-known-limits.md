# Grind Size Known Limits

Grind settings are not physically universal.

Known limits:

- Burr zero point can differ across units.
- Burr alignment, seasoning, retention, and cleanliness change results.
- Grinder charts may use different calibration conventions.
- Espresso cannot be guaranteed without dose, yield, shot time, pressure, puck prep, and real taste feedback.
- Water, roast age, bean density, and processing can shift extraction needs.
- Dataset-unverified grinders stay low confidence until public source or calibration data is added.

Safe product copy:

> AI Brew and Grind Size give a strong starting point and correction direction. Dial in with real taste and change one variable at a time.
